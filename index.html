Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Data We Collect

  • Email address — for account creation and authentication only
  • Username — displayed to other players in your game
  • Location data — during active gameplay only, not stored after game ends
  • In-game chat messages — stored per game session

How We Use It

Your location is used solely to power gameplay mechanics (boundary enforcement, proximity detection). It is broadcast only to players in your active game and is not retained after the game concludes.

Email is used for login only. We do not send marketing emails.

Third Parties

  • Supabase — authentication and secure data storage
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers

Children's Privacy

Hide and Seek Live! is intended for users 13 and older. Teen accounts are restricted to private, invite-only games. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us below. Upon deletion, your location history, chat messages, and profile data are permanently removed.
