Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026 • Last updated: May 21, 2026

Hide and Seek Live! ("we", "us", or "our") is a real-world location-based game. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used and protected. By using the app, you agree to these practices.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you register, we collect your email address, username, and the authentication provider you used (email/password, Sign in with Apple, or Google Sign-In).

Precise GPS location: During an active game, your device's GPS coordinates are collected and shared in real time with other players in your game. Location data is not collected when you are not in an active game session.

Background location: If you minimize the app during an active game, your location continues to be tracked so other players can find you. This requires the "Always" location permission on iOS. You will be prompted to grant this permission before starting a game.

Photos (Social Mode only): In Social Mode games, the game may prompt players to upload photos as part of gameplay challenges. Photos are stored securely and associated with the game session. They are deleted after the game ends (see Section 5).

Usage data: We collect non-personal data such as game outcomes, feature usage, and crash reports to improve the app.

3. Location Data

Your location is only shared during active games. We do not track, store, or sell your location data outside of game sessions. When a game ends, real-time location sharing stops immediately. Location history for a game session is retained for up to 7 days for anti-cheat review, then permanently deleted.

You may revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings. Doing so will prevent gameplay from functioning correctly.
